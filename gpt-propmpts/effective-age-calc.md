### Effective Age Calculation

#### Building Sizes

```
Building 1: 4000sf, 2001
Building 2: 500sf, 2006
Building 3: 5000sf, 2011
Building 4: 5000sf, 2011
Building 5: 5000sf, 2014
```

`Total: 19,500sf`

#### Building Sizes Percentages:

```
Building 1: 4000 / 19500 = 20.5%
Building 2: 500 / 19500 = 2.56%
Building 3: 5000 / 19500 = 25.64%
Building 4: 5000 / 19500 = 25.64%
Building 5: 5000 / 19500 = 25.64%
```

#### How old are the buildings in 2025?

```
Building 1: 2025 - 2001 = 24 years
Building 2: 2025 - 2006 = 19 years
Building 3: 2025 - 2011 = 14 years
Building 4: 2025 - 2011 = 14 years
Building 5: 2025 - 2014 = 11 years
```

#### Multiply the effective age by the percentage of the total square footage for each building:

```
Building 1: 24 years * 20.5% = 4.92 years
Building 2: 19 years * 2.56% = 0.4864 years
Building 3: 14 years * 25.64% = 3.5896 years
Building 4: 14 years * 25.64% = 3.5896 years
Building 5: 11 years * 25.64% = 2.8204 years
```

#### Add the results together:

```
4.92 + 0.4864 + 3.5896 + 3.5896 + 2.8204 = 15.326 years
```

**The effective age of the buildings is 15.3 years.**
